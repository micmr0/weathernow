🌦 Weather Now – fast, beautiful and precise weather at a glance.

Weather Now is a modern weather application designed for clarity, speed and accuracy. Everything you need is presented on a single, clean screen — no unnecessary navigation.

With Weather Now you can:

🔹 Get instant, real-time forecasts for your location or anywhere in the world

🔹 View hourly and daily weather data in a simple, readable format

🔹 Check air quality, moon information and activity conditions

🔹 Plan your day with confidence – walking, running or cycling
